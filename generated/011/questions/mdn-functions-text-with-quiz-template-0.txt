 What is the result of the following code?
const myText = 'I am a string';
const newString = myText.replace('string', 'sausage');

A) "I am a sausage"
B) "I am a string"
C) "I sausage a string"
D) Syntax error

Correct answer: A) "I am a sausage"

Incorrect answer: B) "I am a string"
Incorrect answer: C) "I sausage a string"
Incorrect answer: D) Syntax error
